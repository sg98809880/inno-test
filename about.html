About Innovex Infratech

Innovation today. Excellence forever.

M/s INNOVEX INFRATECH PRIVATE LIMITED is a next-generation IT infrastructure, software development and digital transformation company headquartered in Bengaluru, Karnataka.

A trusted partner for complete, reliable IT

With a strong focus on innovation, security and long-term customer satisfaction, we provide end-to-end technology services that empower businesses, government offices and educational institutions to operate efficiently, securely and seamlessly.

We embrace new ideas, deliver reliable solutions, and build for a future where quality stands the test of time — supporting mission-critical operations 24x7x365 with the same level of care whether the client is a government department, a growing enterprise, or an educational campus.

Our domain focus on IT infrastructure, data centers and networking is backed by audits and post-implementation support — not a one-time install and a goodbye.
