Frequently asked questions

Answers before you ask

Which industries do you primarily serve?

We work across Government offices, corporate and enterprise clients, and educational institutions — with experience in audits and post-implementation compliance support that government and regulated clients typically require.

Do you offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)?

Yes. AMC coverage spans IT infrastructure, networking and security assets, with 24x7 monitoring, patch management and SLA-based incident response.

Can you support a hybrid or multi-cloud environment?

Yes — our cloud and virtualization team designs and migrates workloads across hybrid and multi-cloud setups, including DRaaS, VDI and containerization with Docker and Kubernetes.

Do you provide IT staffing in addition to infrastructure work?

Yes. Our manpower supply and workforce deployment service places software, IT, telecom and electronics professionals on project, contract or long-term terms, with rapid mobilization where needed.

What compliance standards can you help us work toward?

Our cyber security and compliance team supports audits and documentation aligned to ISO 27001, GDPR and HIPAA, alongside VAPT and risk assessments.

Do you handle both new deployments and existing-network upgrades?

Both. We design new data centers and networks from scratch, and we also audit and upgrade aging infrastructure already in place.
